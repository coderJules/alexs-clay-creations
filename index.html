About Alex

At the age of two years, seven months, Alex was diagnosed with autism. Along with his diagnosis came many challenges. but one thing always came easy for Alex was art. When busy working with his hands Alex finds enjoyment and relief from his anxiety. Over the years, he has refined his talent, and developed his own signature style. Once you have seen Alex's work, it is easy to recognize the whimsical personality of one of his pieces.

Awards

Washington, D.C.

Alex participated in an international competition and was one of the three hundred selected to have his art exhibited in Washington, D.C. As a result, he was featured on the front page of the Lansing State Journal.

Local

Most recently Alex's art was chosen to be part of the 17th Annual Ingham Student Art Exhibit. They were exhibited at the Lansing Art Gallery and Bloom Coffee Roasters. His paintings have also been shown throughout Michigan as part of the VSA Michigan Touring Exhibit.

Global

Alex's work has even travelled overseas! Three of his clay figures were sent to Kenya to be given as safari trip souvenirs.
